Combat Veteran. Public Servant. Republican Leader.

Colby Fuller is a proud South Carolina native from the City of Cayce. Raised with humble beginnings, Colby learned early the value of hard work, faith, and service to others.

Immediately after high school, Colby enlisted in the United States Air Force, serving four years including a combat deployment to Iraq supporting Operation Inherent Resolve.

During his service, Colby earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions. After leaving the military, he continued his education earning an MBA and a second Master’s Degree in Management with a focus in Public Administration.

Colby also served three years working with the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.

Now at just 25 years old, Colby is running for South Carolina House District 52 to fight for the working class, support our veterans, and ensure South Carolina families have a stronger future.
