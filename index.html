Meet Colby

Colby Fuller is a combat veteran who served four years in the United States Air Force, including a deployment to Iraq supporting Operation Inherent Resolve.

During his military service he earned a Bachelor's Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions. After leaving the military he continued his education earning an MBA and a Master's in Management focused on Public Administration.

Colby also spent three years serving the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.

At just 25 years old, Colby is running to bring new leadership, new ideas, and renewed energy to Columbia.
