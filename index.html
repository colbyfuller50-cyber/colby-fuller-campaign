Meet Colby

Colby Fuller grew up in the City of Cayce and understands what it means to work hard and earn every opportunity.

Immediately after high school, Colby enlisted in the United States Air Force where he served four years, including a combat deployment to Iraq supporting Operation Inherent Resolve.

During his military service he earned his Bachelor's Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions.

After leaving the military, he continued his education earning an MBA and a second Master's Degree in Management focusing on Public Administration.

Colby also served three years working with the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.

At just 25 years old, Colby is running to bring new leadership, new ideas, and renewed energy to South Carolina.
