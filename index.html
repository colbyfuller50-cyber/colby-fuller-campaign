Meet Colby

Raised in Cayce, South Carolina, Colby Fuller enlisted in the United States Air Force after high school.

He served four years including a deployment to Iraq supporting Operation Inherent Resolve.

While serving he earned a Bachelor's Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions.

He later earned an MBA and a Master's Degree in Management focused on Public Administration.

Colby also served three years working for the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.

At just 25 years old, Colby is running to bring new leadership and renewed energy to South Carolina.
