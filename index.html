Meet Colby

Raised in the City of Cayce, Colby Fuller grew up understanding the value of hard work and service to others.

After high school he enlisted in the United States Air Force, serving four years including a deployment to Iraq in support of Operation Inherent Resolve.

During his service he earned his Bachelor's Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions.

He later earned an MBA and a Master's Degree in Management focused on Public Administration.

Colby also spent three years serving the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.

Now he is running to bring new leadership and renewed energy to Columbia.
