Meet Colby

Raised in Cayce, Colby Fuller grew up understanding the value of service and hard work.

After high school he enlisted in the United States Air Force, serving four years including a deployment to Iraq supporting Operation Inherent Resolve.

During his service he earned his Bachelor's Degree in Government Contracting and Acquisitions.

After leaving the military he continued his education earning an MBA and a Master's in Management focused on Public Administration.

Colby also served three years working with the State of South Carolina before transitioning into the private sector.
