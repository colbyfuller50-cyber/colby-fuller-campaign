My Vision for South Carolina

Support Our Veterans

Those who served our country deserve better. I will fight for expanded veteran healthcare access, workforce programs, and state-level benefits for those who defended our freedoms.

Empower the Working Class

South Carolinians deserve good-paying jobs and economic opportunities. I will work to attract industry, support small business owners, and reduce burdensome regulations.

Responsible Government

Our government must serve the people—not bureaucracy. I will advocate for responsible spending, transparency, and policies that empower communities.

Stronger Communities

Safe communities, strong families, and better education are essential to South Carolina’s future.
